Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top right corner of the page. Fill in your details and select your account type (Job Seeker or Employer).

How do I post a job?

To post a job, you need to register as an employer. After logging in, go to your Employer Dashboard and click on "Post a Job". Fill in the job details and submit the form.

How can I search for jobs?

You can search for jobs using the search bar on the homepage or go to the Job Listings page. You can filter jobs by keywords, location, job type, and category.

Is it free to use JobHub?

Yes, basic features are free for both job seekers and employers. We also offer premium plans with additional features for employers.

How do I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, click on the job listing to view details, then click the "Apply Now" button. You'll need to fill out an application form and upload your resume.
